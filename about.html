I am an undergraduate student driven by a deep curiosity about the Universe and its fundamental structure. My academic interests lie in observational cosmology, with a particular focus on dark matter and the large scale structure of the Universe.

I am especially interested in how galaxy groups and clusters can be used as tracers of underlying dark matter halos, and how observations across different wavelengths help reveal the physical processes shaping cosmic structure.

Alongside coursework and independent study, I actively seek research environments that allow me to engage with real data, develop analytical thinking, and collaborate with researchers who are advancing our understanding of the cosmos.

My long term goal is to pursue graduate studies in physics or astronomy and contribute to fundamental research addressing open questions in cosmology.
